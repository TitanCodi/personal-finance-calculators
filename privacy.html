Last updated: May 1, 2025  ·  PersonalFinanceTools

The short version: We don't collect, store, or sell your personal data. All calculator inputs stay in your browser. We display Google ads which may use cookies — details below.

PersonalFinanceTools operates the website at personalfinancetools.app (the "Site"). We provide free, browser-based financial calculators for personal use.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at hello@personalfinancetools.app.

2Information We Do Not Collect

All calculations on this Site run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit any numbers you enter into our calculators

  • Store your calculation history
  • Sell or share your personal data with third parties

Your financial inputs never leave your device.

Like most websites, we may collect certain non-personal information automatically when you visit the Site, including:

This information is used only in aggregate to understand how visitors use the Site and to improve our calculators.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this Site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our Site, please visit: How Google uses data from sites that use Google services.

5Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the Site to function properly.

  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant advertisements. These may track your browsing across other websites.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the display of advertisements but will not affect calculator functionality.
